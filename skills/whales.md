# Whale Tracking Skill

## Purpose
Monitor and analyze large wallet movements to identify smart money activity.

## Whale Categories

### Tier 1 Whales
- Wallet value > $1,000,000
- Portfolio value > $10,000,000
- Professional traders and funds

### Tier 2 Whales  
- Wallet value > $100,000
- Active on multiple chains
- Consistent profit history

### Smart Money Indicators
- Early token accumulation (before price rise)
- Consistent buying during dips
- Profit-taking at resistance
- Low portfolio turnover (long-term holders)

## Tracking Metrics

### Buy Signals
- Large accumulation (>5% of supply increase)
- Steady buying over 4+ hours
- Buying at support levels
- Cross-chain diversification buys

### Sell Signals
- Distribution pattern (gradual selling)
- Selling into strength
- Large transfers to exchanges
- Portfolio rebalancing

### Watch Patterns
- Wallet creation and funding
- Exchange wallet movements
- Cross-chain bridge activity
- Contract interactions

## Whale Watch Rules
- Alert on Tier 1 movements > $50,000
- Alert on Tier 2 movements > $10,000
- Track exchange wallet flows daily
- Monitor smart money wallet addresses
- Detect whale vs retail flow ratio

## Smart Money Score
Calculate score based on:
- Accumulation timing accuracy
- Profit ratio over 30 days
- Diversification strategy
- Risk management patterns
